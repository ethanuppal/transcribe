Welcome to the song library!

Here you'll find many recordings—some even with precreated sections!—that you can use so you don't have to find your own. To request that songs be added to the library, send me a message on GitHub.

If you're wondering how to transcribe, JazzAdvice.com is an excellent resource. This post, for example, has a wealth of information and links to many others.
